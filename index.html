Invictus Edge is an experimental research platform that applies cycle-based timing context to financial market analysis. It is not a trading signal service, and it does not provide financial advice.

Public Scope

The public dashboard shows summarized scores and high-level context only. Private research rules, weighting, broker execution logic, and backtest implementation details are intentionally kept out of the public UI.
